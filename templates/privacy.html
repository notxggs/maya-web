Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

1. What we collect

When you use Maya's commands, we may store:

  • Server (guild) IDs, and per-server settings like your command prefix and 24/7 mode preference
  • Song/queue history used to power features like the server leaderboard

When you log into the dashboard via Discord OAuth2, we additionally receive and store:

  • Your Discord user ID, username, and avatar
  • The list of servers you're in, so we can show you which ones Maya is also in

2. What we don't collect

Maya does not read, log, or store the contents of your Discord messages. We don't collect payment information — Maya has no paid features.

3. Cookies and sessions

The dashboard sets a single session cookie (rose_session) after you log in with Discord. It's used only to keep you signed in and is not shared with third parties or used for tracking/advertising.

4. How we use this data

Data is used only to operate Maya's features: running commands, serving the dashboard, syncing playback state in real time, and enforcing permission levels (owner, manager, member) in each server.

5. Third parties

Maya communicates with Discord's API to operate, and with third-party audio sources (such as YouTube and other providers) to fetch songs you request. These providers have their own privacy policies governing how they handle requests made to them.

6. Data retention and deletion

Server settings and queue history are retained for as long as Maya remains in your server. If Maya is removed from a server, its associated settings and history are eventually deleted. You can request earlier deletion of your data by contacting us through the support server.

7. Children's privacy

Maya is intended for use in accordance with Discord's own age requirements and is not directed at children under the age required by Discord's Terms of Service.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Maya after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy or a data deletion request? Run support in any server with Maya to get an invite to the support server.
